MAY MAI LUOI DAO RANG CUA

Bo Dieu Khien DMC + Man Hinh Cam Ung HMI — Huong Dan Quy Trinh Van Hanh

▮ DMC = Bo Dieu Khien (vien dam)
▮ HMI = Man Hinh (nen xam)
▮ NGUOI DUNG = Thao Tac (gach net)
▮ TU DONG = Tu Dong (den)
1. KHOI DONG & BAT MAY

Nhung gi xay ra khi ban bat may va mo ung dung HMI

1
Bat nguon bo dieu khien Galil
Bo dieu khien chay #AUTO ngay lap tuc: xoa dau ra, tai cau hinh, cai dat thong so, khai bao mang, ve goc tat ca cac truc, khoi dong luong 2.
TU DONGDMC #AUTO
|
2
DMC vao vong cho chinh
Sau khi ve goc, DMC cho tai #WtAtRt kiem tra nut bam va bien HMI moi 100ms. Trang thai = CHO (IDLE - 1).
DMC #MAINhmiState=1
|
3
Mo ung dung man hinh cam ung HMI
Ung dung bat dau o man hinh dang nhap. Thanh trang thai hien thi NGOAI TUYEN (OFFLINE) cho den khi ket noi.
HMINGUOI DUNG
|
4
Dang nhap & chon loai may
Nhap ma PIN. Chon loai may neu lan dau (Mai Phang 4 Truc, Mai Loi 4 Truc, hoac Mai Rang Cua 3 Truc).
NGUOI DUNGMan Hinh Dang Nhap
|
5
Ket noi voi bo dieu khien
Nhap dia chi IP bo dieu khien va nhan Ket Noi. HMI bat dau doc du lieu 10 lan/giay. Thanh trang thai chuyen sang DA KET NOI + hien thi trang thai hien tai (CHO).
NGUOI DUNGMan Hinh Cai DatBat dau doc
2. CHE DO CAI DAT (Truoc Khi Mai Lan Dau)

Cau hinh may cho loai dao ban dang mai

1
Chuyen den man hinh Cai Dat Truc
HMI tu dong gui hmiSetp=0 de vao che do cai dat. DMC vao #SULOOP. Trang thai = CAI DAT (SETUP - 3).
NGUOI DUNGhmiSetp=0DMC #SETUP
|
2
Di chuyen cac truc den vi tri
Su dung cac nut di chuyen tren HMI de di chuyen tung truc (A, B, C, D). HMI gui lenh PR{truc}=xung; BG{truc} truc tiep. Vi tri cap nhat truc tiep 10 lan/giay.
NGUOI DUNGCai Dat TrucLenh PR/BG
|
3
Luu vi tri NGHI (REST)
Di chuyen den vi tri dau an toan, nhan "Dat Vi Tri Nghi". HMI doc vi tri _TD{truc} hien tai, ghi vao restPtA/B/C/D, luu vao EEPROM bang BV.
NGUOI DUNGNut DayrestPt* + BV
|
4
Luu vi tri BAT DAU (START)
Di chuyen den diem bat dau mai (dau luoi dao), nhan "Dat Vi Tri Bat Dau". HMI ghi startPtA/B/C/D va luu bang BV.
NGUOI DUNGNut DaystartPt* + BV
|
5
Thao tac nhanh: Ve Goc / Den Nghi / Den Bat Dau
Ve Goc Tat Ca: hmiHome=0 → DMC chay #HOME (trang thai ngan = DANG VE GOC).
Den Vi Tri Nghi: hmiGoRs=0 → DMC chay #GOREST.
Den Vi Tri Bat Dau: hmiGoSt=0 → DMC chay #GOSTR.
NGUOI DUNGThao Tac NhanhBien kich hoat
|
6
Chinh sua thong so (tuy chon)
Chuyen den man hinh Thong So (van o che do cai dat). Dieu chinh do day dao, toc do, hieu chuan. Nhan Ap Dung → HMI ghi bien va luu BV. DMC tinh lai gia tri cpm* qua hmiCalc=0.
NGUOI DUNGMan Hinh Thong SoGhi bien + BV
|
7
Tai/luu ho so (tuy chon)
Vao man hinh Ho So. Nhap CSV de tai ho so dao da luu, hoac xuat cai dat hien tai. Nhap hien thi hop thoai so sanh truoc khi ap dung.
NGUOI DUNGMan Hinh Ho So
|
8
Chuyen den man hinh VAN HANH (RUN)
HMI gui hmiExSt=0 de thoat cai dat. DMC tro ve tu #SETUP ve #MAIN. Trang thai = CHO (IDLE - 1). May san sang mai.
NGUOI DUNGhmiExSt=0Ve #MAIN
3. CHU TRINH MAI

Chay qua trinh mai — nguoi van hanh theo doi tu man hinh RUN

1
Nhan BAT DAU MAI
HMI gui hmiGrnd=0. DMC vao #GRIND. Trang thai = DANG MAI (GRINDING - 2). HMI tat nut Bat Dau, hien nut DUNG.
NGUOI DUNGhmiGrnd=0DMC #GRIND
|
2
DMC thuc hien trinh tu mai
Giai doan 1: Di chuyen den vi tri bat dau (#GOSTR), dat goc D + bu tru C
Giai doan 2: Duong mai — nap cac doan deltaA/B/C/D qua Noi Suy Tuyen Tinh (LI)
Giai doan 3: A vuot qua got dao, dau ve vi tri nghi (#GOREST)
DMC #GRINDTU DONG
|
3
HMI theo doi truc tiep
Bieu do vi tri A/B cap nhat 5 lan/giay. So luong dao tang. Thanh trang thai hien thi DANG MAI. Nguoi van hanh theo doi tien trinh.
Man Hinh RUNDoc 10 lan/giay
|
4
Mai hoan thanh
DMC tang ctSesKni va ctStnKni, dau ve vi tri nghi, tro ve #MAIN. Trang thai = CHO (IDLE - 1). HMI kich hoat lai nut Bat Dau. Dat dao tiep theo va lap lai.
DMC #DONEhmiState=1
4. TRONG KHI MAI — Cac Thao Tac Co The Thuc Hien

Nhung gi nguoi van hanh co the lam khi dang mai

A
DUNG (tam dung nhe)
HMI gui ST ABCD truc tiep. Tat ca cac truc dung. Chuong trinh DMC van chay. Dung de tam dung giua chu trinh.
NGUOI DUNGNut DUNGST ABCD
|
B
THEM DA / BOT DA
hmiMore=0 hoac hmiLess=0. DMC dieu chinh bu tru da mai tang 0.001mm. HMI doc lai gia tri vi tri va cap nhat hien thi.
NGUOI DUNGNut Da Mai#MOREGRI / #LESSGRI
|
C
Dieu chinh bu tru Delta-C (chi Mai Phang/Loi)
Nhan vao bieu do cot de dieu chinh do sau mai theo tung phan. Nhan AP DUNG de tai mang deltaC[] xuong bo dieu khien.
NGUOI DUNGBieu Do Delta-CMang deltaC[]
|
D
!!! DUNG KHAN CAP (E-STOP) !!!
HMI gui ST ABCD + HX (dung chuong trinh). Tat ca chuyen dong dung, chuong trinh bi huy. Thanh trang thai hien thi DUNG KHAN CAP. Can PHUC HOI de khoi dong lai.
NGUOI DUNGNut E-STOPST + HX
5. PHUC HOI SAU DUNG KHAN CAP

Cach khoi dong lai sau khi dung khan cap

1
DUNG KHAN CAP dang kich hoat
Thanh trang thai hien thi DUNG KHAN CAP. Tat ca cac truc da dung. Chuong trinh DMC da dung. Nut PHUC HOI xuat hien tren thanh trang thai.
TU DONGThanh Trang Thai
|
2
Nhan PHUC HOI
HMI gui SH ABCD (bat servo) + XQ #AUTO (khoi dong lai chuong trinh tu dau). Day la lenh XQ duy nhat duoc phep. DMC khoi tao lai: cau hinh, thong so, ve goc, roi vao #MAIN.
NGUOI DUNGNut PHUC HOIXQ #AUTO
|
3
He thong phuc hoi
DMC ve goc tat ca cac truc, vao CHO. Thanh trang thai hien thi CHO (IDLE). May san sang van hanh binh thuong. Dat lai vi tri bat dau/nghi neu can.
TU DONG#AUTO → #HOME → #MAIN
6. PHIEN MOI (Thay Da Mai)

Khi ban thay da mai moi

1
Nhan PHIEN MOI
HMI gui hmiNewS=0. DMC chay #NEWSESS: ve goc tat ca cac truc, dat lai ca hai bo dem dao (ctSesKni=0, ctStnKni=0).
NGUOI DUNGhmiNewS=0#NEWSESS
|
2
Dat lai cac vi tri
Da mai moi co the co kich thuoc khac. Vao Cai Dat Truc, di chuyen den vi tri moi, dat lai vi tri NGHI va BAT DAU.
NGUOI DUNGCai Dat Truc

Dieu Huong Man Hinh HMI

Cach cac man hinh cam ung ket noi voi cac che do DMC

Man Hinh HMI
  • Dang Nhap — Nhap ma PIN, chon loai may
  • Cai Dat — IP bo dieu khien, ket noi/ngat ket noi
  • VAN HANH (RUN) — Bat dau/Dung mai, bu tru da, delta-C, bieu do
  • Cai Dat Truc — Di chuyen truc, dat vi tri nghi/bat dau
  • Thong So — Do day dao, toc do, hieu chuan
  • Ho So — Nhap/xuat ho so dao CSV
  • Thanh Trang Thai — Luon hien thi: trang thai, ket noi, nut E-STOP
Che Do DMC
  • #MAIN / #WtAtRt — Vong cho, kiem tra bien kich hoat
  • #GRIND — Chu trinh mai day du (bat dau, duong mai, nghi)
  • #SETUP / #SULOOP — Menu cai dat, kiem tra bien cai dat
  • #HOME — Trinh tu ve goc 4 truc
  • #GOREST / #GOSTR — Di chuyen an toan nhieu truc
  • #VARCALC — Tinh lai gia tri tu thong so
  • #MOREGRI / #LESSGRI — Dieu chinh bu tru da mai
⚠ MAN HINH CAI DAT CUNG NHOM: Chuyen giua Cai Dat Truc va Thong So KHONG thoat che do cai dat (khong gui hmiExSt=0). Che do cai dat chi thoat khi chuyen den man hinh khong phai cai dat (VAN HANH, Ho So, v.v.).
